About Us

Student Math Mentors is a student-run volunteer tutoring program offering free tutoring for students from 1st to 8th grade. We believe in the power of education and aim to help students excel in their math skills.

Our Services

  • One-on-one tutoring sessions
  • Group study sessions
  • Homework help
  • Test preparation

Get Tutoring

If you are a student in need of math tutoring, we are here to help! Our tutors are available to assist with a variety of math topics and grade levels.

Student Resources

We provide a variety of resources to help students improve their math skills, including practice worksheets, video tutorials, and interactive games.

Volunteer with Us

Join our team of dedicated student tutors and make a difference in your community. Volunteer with us to help younger students improve their math skills and build confidence.

About Us

Student Math Mentors is a student-run volunteer tutoring program offering free tutoring for students from 1st to 8th grade. We believe in the power of education and aim to help students excel in their math skills.

Our Services

We offer a variety of tutoring services to help students improve their math skills, including one-on-one tutoring sessions, group study sessions, homework help, and test preparation.

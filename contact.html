Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your message subject line.
Is tutoring really free?
Yes! Student Math Mentors is a volunteer-run program. All of our tutoring services are completely free for students in grades 1-8.
How are students matched with tutors?
Both sides provide us with scheduling and their respective math levels, and our team works to make sure each student is paired with the tutor who best suits their skills and availability.
What if I want to become a tutor?
That's great! Please visit our volunteer page to fill out the tutor application form, or select "Volunteer Application" in the subject dropdown above.
